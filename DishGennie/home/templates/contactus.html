Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about DishGennie and our services.

How does DishGennie's AI recipe generator work?
Our AI analyzes thousands of recipes, your dietary preferences, and available ingredients to create personalized meal suggestions. The more you use it, the better it understands your tastes and cooking style.
Is there a free version of DishGennie?
Yes! We offer a free tier with basic meal planning and recipe generation. For advanced features like nutritional analysis, shopping list automation, and premium recipes, check out our Pro and Family plans.
How accurate is the nutritional information?
Our nutritional data comes from verified sources and is calculated based on standard ingredient measurements. For precise dietary needs, we recommend consulting with a nutritionist, but our data is accurate for general meal planning.
Can I share my meal plans with family members?
Absolutely! With our Family plan, you can share meal plans, shopping lists, and recipes with up to 6 family members. Everyone can contribute to the meal planning process.
